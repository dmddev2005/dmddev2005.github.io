Rules & Club Guidelines

Basic match rules + club etiquette. Keep this page as the official reference.

All Match Basics

  • Arrive at least 30 min early before match start time.
  • Warm-up is mandatory.
  • Helmet is mandatory to face fast bowling.
  • Keep a first-aid kit with the team.
  • All club equipment must be returned to the kit immediately after use. Club jerseys must be returned after the match.
  • Do not argue with umpires
  • Follow playing condition per umpire decision (Rain, wet field etc).
  • See YCAC guidelines on Cricket.

Membership and Match Fees
